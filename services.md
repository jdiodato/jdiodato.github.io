### Tutoring 
In general, I work with students across a variety of subject areas at the K-8 level. Below I've highlighted specific areas that I am quite passionate about!

- **Mathematics**: Math is a beautiful subject, and I strive to help students see that beauty through each problem that we tackle. I am currently accepting students for math tutoring in the following areas:
  - K-8th Grade Math
  - Pre-Algebra
  - Algebra 1 and Algebra 2
  - Geometry
- **English**: As a voracious reader, I take a great deal of pride in working with students of all ages and levels on their reading comprehension, grammar skills, or any other areas pertaining to the English language. Whether your main goals are in reading or writing, let me help you or your student develop a firmer grasp of English! 
- **Python (Computer Programming)**: Computer programming is an incredibly exciting subject, and I believe that Python is one of the best ways to introduce students to this wonderful new world. I currently use Python's [Turtle module](https://docs.python.org/3.3/library/turtle.html?highlight=turtle) to provide students with a fun and visual introduction to code! I'm currently accepting computer programming students who are new to Python and possibly ready to start their first class. 

**Tutoring Rate: $45 (in-person)/ $30 (virtually)**: I am now offering socially-distanced in-person tutoring sessions, as well virtual options through Zoom or Skype.
