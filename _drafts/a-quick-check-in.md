It's been quite some time since I've posted here! I'm hoping to do a better of job of keeping up with my blog, since I've recently embraced a creative burst of energy to resume writing.

I returned home to New York City in March of this year, as colleges and universities across the country were shutting down to curtail the spread of the novel coronavirus. My time as a graduate student at Indiana University was quickly winding down at the time, and so I took the opportunity to return home.

I currently work as a crew member at a Dunkin' to stay on top of my finances. My full-time job search fizzled out a bit as I quickly realized how much energy was being sapped by being back in food service. That's not to say that it's ground to a _complete_ halt, but I wish that I had maintained more momentum looking back over the last few months.

Still, I'm happy to be in good health and keeping up with pursuits like music that are important to me. As my time separated from higher education administration slowly increases, I hope that reading, seeking professional development, and keeping up with this blog will lessen that feeling of distance. 

Thanks for reading,

Joe
