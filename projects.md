Below are some projects that I am currently working on.

## 1. The Open HESA Project
Inspired by open educational resources and YouTube channels in the style of Salman Khan's Khan Academy, I aspire to curate a learning community of open resources related to higher education and student affairs administration. Topics may focus more on areas that I am particularly passionate about, including quantitative research methods, computing tools (Python, R, bash, etc.), and program assessment/and evaluation.

## 2. SA Game Dev Group
I'm part of a social media group of gamers that work in higher education and student affairs, and I've received a decent amount of interest in creating a digital subgroup of folks that want to learn more about game development. I'm currently in the planning phases of this project, but it will for sure include materials that are only freely available.
