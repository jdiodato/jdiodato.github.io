This page outlines some resources and tools that I use in my work, as well as a few links that I'm quite fond of. It is also a living document, so please check back often. The links below are generally related to higher education, data, and/or computing.

**General**
- [Github Pages](https://pages.github.com/): My preferred tool to develop personal/professional websites or portfolios. It's not everyone'e cup of tea, but definitely worth a look.
- [R programming language](https://www.r-project.org/about.html)
- [RStudio](https://www.rstudio.com/), an integrated development environment (IDE), that provides a very convenient way to work with the R language.

**Data**
- [Integrated Postsecondary Education Data System](https://nces.ed.gov/ipeds/), also known as IPEDS.
- [Data.gov](https://www.data.gov/) is a repository of data sets from a number of areas. 
- An [online web scraping tutorial](https://recycledrobot.co.uk/words/?web-scraping) that uses [Python](https://www.python.org/) and a library called [Beautiful Soup](https://www.crummy.com/software/BeautifulSoup/bs4/doc/).
- [r/dataisbeautiful](https://www.reddit.com/r/dataisbeautiful/)
- [r/datasets](https://www.reddit.com/r/datasets/)
- [The Carnegie Classification of Institutions of Higher Education](http://carnegieclassifications.iu.edu/)
- [gapminder](https://www.gapminder.org/): A compilation of economic, medical, and social statistics from around the world.

**Fun Fact**: Did you know that there is a [Squirrel Census](https://www.thesquirrelcensus.com/)? It's probably one of my favorite data sets and non-profits. Take a look if you feel so inclined.

**Miscellaneous**
- [XKCD](https://xkcd.com/): A webcomic that I am quite fond of.
- [The first ever website](http://info.cern.ch/hypertext/WWW/TheProject.html)
- The [Bloomington Pipers' Society](www.bloomingtonpipers.org): In addition to playing with the Society, I also serve as the webmaster and manage the Society's social accounts. Be sure to check out [our Facebook page!](https://www.facebook.com/bloomingtonpipers/)
